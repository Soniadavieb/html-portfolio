The Best Movies According to Sonia

Here are the top 3 movies movies we love at home:

1. Mufasa

We love Mufasa, it is a great story with great music starring Beyonce and daughter Blue Ivy Carter.
It is a great movie for kids and adults alike. The characters are well-developed and the story is engaging.
We love the music and the animation and would love to watch this in 3D.
We love the message of the movie and the way it teaches us about life and love.

2. It takes two

We love it because we love the Olsen twins. It is an original and entertaining movie. They don't make original films with a great storyline like this anymore. It is a fanfastic family movie.

3. The parent trap

Another movie with identical twins starring Lyndsay Lohan, except they are not real twins but play double roles. We love the songs in this movie, a classic favourite that feels more like a musical.
